Links to Helpful Resources

Here you will find links to any website that I find essential or helpful.

  • Cubedesk, a free, open-source cube timer with a ton of features.

  • SpeedCubeShop, an amazing cube shop located in Nevada.

  • The Cubicle, another amazing cube shop, this time located in New York.

  • Cube Academy, an amazing tutorial website made by CubeHead, one of the best cubetubers ever.

  • JPerm.net, a website similar to Cube Academy, but made by another amazing cubetuber, J Perm.
